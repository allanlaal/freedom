fCalendar - a better way to export Facebook events!

 
Why this service?

Facebook events can be syncronized with the calendar on your phone, computer etc. This is neat. However, if you are like me, you also get flooded with invitations to events you don't really care about.

This service filters the calendar feed from Facebook and only shows those events you have marked as "attending" and "maybe attending"; removing those you haven't replied to yet.

This way, you calendar will always stay tidy, and show events which are relevant to you

How to find your Facebook feed

1. Open https://www.facebook.com/events/list

2. Click the options icon

3. Click "Export"

4. Copy the "upcoming events" link and paste it into the field above

5. Download your generated feed, or add it directly to Google Calendar

6. Wait up to 15 minutes for your events to show up in Google Calendar
